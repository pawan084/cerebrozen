CereBro is one calm space for daily mental fitness, better sleep, and calmer focus. It’s proactive but never noisy, private by design, and always honest about what it is — a supportive AI companion, not a therapist or crisis service.

TALK IT THROUGH
A voice and text AI companion that listens, reflects your feelings back, and runs real activities inline — breathing, grounding, and guided journaling — instead of just chatting. Tap once and talk; it hears you, thinks, and speaks back.

SLEEP DEEPER
Layer soundscapes — rain, ocean, wind, and a soft drone — each at its own level, with a sleep-safe timer that gently fades out so you can drift off without your phone.

REFLECT PRIVATELY
A blank-page journal with gentle daily prompts and emotion tags. Optionally lock it behind Face ID. Reflections stay yours; you choose what the AI can read.

BUILD A GENTLE HABIT
A forgiving “mindful days” streak that celebrates milestones without pressure, a single clear next step each day tuned to the time and your goals, and an optional daily nudge.

PRIVACY & SAFETY FIRST
Explicit consent controls what CereBro remembers. Export or delete everything anytime. A persistent reminder that this is wellness support — with locale-aware crisis resources always one tap away, and an optional trusted contact you can choose to notify.

CereBro is not a medical device and does not diagnose, treat, or provide emergency care. If you are in crisis, contact your local emergency services.
