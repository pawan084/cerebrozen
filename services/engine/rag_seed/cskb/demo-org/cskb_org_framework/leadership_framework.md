# Demo Org — leadership framework (illustrative)

> Placeholder CSKB framework for the tenant `demo-org`. Swap for the client's real
> competency model / leadership framework.

Demo Org describes leadership in three horizons a manager is expected to hold at
once:

**Deliver (now).** Set clear outcomes, unblock the team, and close the loop. The
test: everyone can name the one thing that matters most this week and how it's
measured.

**Develop (the quarter).** Grow the people faster than the workload grows. The
test: someone on your team did something this quarter they couldn't have done last
quarter, and they can tell you what you did to make that happen.

**Shape (the year).** Improve the system, not just the output — the process, the
hiring bar, the standards that outlast any single project. The test: the team is
better at working than it was, independent of what it shipped.

A common failure is a strong *Deliver* leader who never leaves it — heroically
shipping while the team stays junior and the system stays broken. Coaching often
moves a client from Deliver into Develop and Shape.
