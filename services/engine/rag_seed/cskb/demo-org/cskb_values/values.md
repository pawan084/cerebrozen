# Demo Org — company values (illustrative)

> Placeholder CSKB content for the tenant `demo-org`. Real deployments upload the
> client's actual values so coaching grounds in *their* language, not generic
> advice ("Tuned to Your Culture").

**1. Default to ownership.** If you see it, it's yours until it's someone else's by
agreement. We don't wait to be assigned the obvious.

**2. Disagree, decide, commit.** We argue hard *before* a decision and row
together after it. Re-litigating a made decision in the hallway is not allowed;
raising new information is.

**3. Respect is the baseline, not the reward.** Candour and kindness are not a
trade-off here. We say the hard thing, and we say it to the person, early.

**4. Bias to the smallest real step.** A shipped rough version beats a perfect
plan. We'd rather learn on Tuesday than be right in a quarter.

**5. Protect the focus time.** Meetings are a cost we choose deliberately. No
agenda, no meeting; no decision or learning, no recurrence.
